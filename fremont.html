City of Fremont Website

Fremont is a city in California's San Francisco Bay Area. The Niles Essanay Silent Film Museum traces the early days of filmmaking through artifacts and photos. Mission San José is a 1700s Spanish Mission, with a reconstructed church and a museum. The Ardenwood Historic Farm demonstrates horse-powered farming, and features a Victorian mansion. Central Park is home to Lake Elizabeth and the Aqua Adventure Water Park.

Fun Facts

  • Fremont, CA is a diverse and innovative city in Silicon Valley, offering beautiful parks, historic districts, and top-notch education, making it a great place for families and tech professionals.
  • With an area of approximately 92.52 square miles, Fremont is one of the largest cities in California.
  • As part of the historic California Missions Trail, Fremont is home to the beautifully preserved Mission San Jose, founded in 1797.
  • Fremont’s dining scene offers a diverse range of culinary options, from authentic ethnic eateries to trendy modern restaurants.
  • Mission Peak, one of the iconic peaks in the Bay Area, offers panoramic views of Fremont and the surrounding region.
