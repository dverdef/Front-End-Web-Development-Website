City of Union City Website

Union City is a city in Alameda County, California, United States in the San Francisco Bay Area. It is located approximately 19 miles south of Oakland, 30 miles from San Francisco, and 20 miles north of San Jose. It was incorporated in 1959, combining the communities of Alvarado and Decoto.

Summary from Wikipedia

Fun Facts

  • Tucked between Hayward and Fremont in the east bay of San Francisco,Union City became a popular destination for former gold miners.
  • The area was considered to have the most fertile land in the state and became an agricultural region during the 19th century.
  • Today, Union City retains its small-town feel and provides a comfortable suburban environment with access to the amenities of the Bay Area.
  • Union City is also home to a variety of restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues, offering something forveryone.
  • Spanning over 19 square miles, Union City is home to a variety of ethnic backgrounds and cultures.
